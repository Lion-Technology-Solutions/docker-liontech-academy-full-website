About LionTech Academy

Transforming Tech Education Since 2015

LionTech Academy is a premier technology training institution specializing in DevOps and Cloud computing education. With campuses across Canada and Africa, we provide hands-on, industry-relevant training to help students succeed in the rapidly evolving tech landscape.

Our expert instructors bring real-world experience to the classroom, ensuring you gain practical skills that employers value. Whether you're just starting your tech journey or looking to upskill, LionTech Academy has the perfect program for you.

At LionTech, we believe in learning by doing. Our curriculum emphasizes practical projects and real-world scenarios to prepare you for the challenges of today's tech industry.
